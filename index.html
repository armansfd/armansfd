About

I am currently with the Wireless Research Team at BT Labs, focusing on massive MIMO, disaggregated RAN, cell-less antenna systems, and applications of AI for 5G and beyond. I am also a 3GPP RAN1 Delegate for BT, contributing to the standardisation of 5G NR. I previously held post-doctoral research positions at University College London and University of Manchester. 

Arman has an extensive experience of working in collaborative research projects including EPSRC UPFRONT, EPSRC Green HetNets, GreenTouch Consortium, and ICT FP7 ACROPOLIS, with over 45 technical paper publications in prestigious IEEE journals and conferences. He was a recipient of the Exemplary Reviewer Certificate of the IEEE Wireless Communications Letters in 2015 and the IEEE Transactions on Communications in 2017. He is currently serving an Associate Editor for the IET Electronics Letters.

I have a B.Eng. degree in Information Systems Engineering from Imperial College London, UK, an M.Sc. degree in Signal Processing (with Distinction), together with a Ph.D. degree in Telecommunications from King's College London, UK.
