About

+7 years of work experience in R&D of transmission technologies and architectures for 4G LTE/LTE-A, 5G NR, and beyond. 

I am currently with InterDigital where I lead the CELTIC-NEXT European collaborative project on AIMM (AI-enabled Massive MIMO): https://www.celticnext.eu/project-aimm/

I was previously a Wireless Research Manager and 3GPP RAN1 WG Delegate at British Telecoms Labs. Prior, I held post-doctoral research positions at University College London and University of Manchester.

I have a BEng degree in Information Systems Engineering from Imperial College London, an MSc degree in Signal Processing (with Distinction) together with a PhD degree in Telecommunications from King's College London. 

Currently an Editor for IEEE Wireless Communications Letters and IET Electronics Letters.
