ARMAN SHOJAEIFARD, Ph.D., M.Sc., B.Eng. 

Bio

I am a Research Fellow at the Communications and Information Systems Group, University College London. My research is in the area of Information Theory and Communications, with a particular focus on the design, modelling, performance analysis, and optimisation of 5G and beyond cellular networks.
