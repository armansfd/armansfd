About

I am with the Wireless Research Team at BT Labs, focusing on massive MIMO, disaggregated RAN, and applications of AI for 5G and beyond. My background is in applied probability theory, optimisation techniques, and system-level simulations for wireless networks.

I have a B.Eng. degree in Information Systems Engineering from Imperial College London, UK, an M.Sc. degree in Signal Processing (with Distinction), together with a Ph.D. degree in Telecommunications from King's College London, UK.
