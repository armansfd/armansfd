About

I am a Senior Researcher with BT Research and Innovation. My background is in stochastic modelling and analysis, applied probability theory, optimisation techniques, and system-level simulations for large-scale networks.

I have a B.Eng. degree in Information Systems Engineering from Imperial College London, UK, an M.Sc. degree in Signal Processing (with Distinction), together with a Ph.D. degree in Wireless Communications from King's College London, UK.
