About

I am currently with BT Labs where I lead the CELTIC-NEXT European collaborative project on AIMM (AI-enabled Massive MIMO): https://www.celticnext.eu/project-aimm/

My responsbilities also include represnting BT at RAN standardisation bodies including 3GPP RAN1 WG and O-RAN Alliance WG3. 

I have an extensive experience of working in collaborative research projects including EPSRC UPFRONT, EPSRC Green HetNets, GreenTouch Consortium, and ICT FP7 ACROPOLIS with over 45 technical IEEE paper publications, >550 citations and an h-index of 13. I am currently an Editor for IEEE Wireless Communications Letters and IET Electronics Letters. 

I have a BEng degree in Information Systems Engineering from Imperial College London, an MSc degree in Signal Processing (with Distinction) together with a PhD degree in Telecommunications from King's College London. I previously held post-doctoral research positions at University College London and University of Manchester.
